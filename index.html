About Us

At A.P.A, our goal is to provide users with safe and up-to-date APK files. We automatically fetch all APKs using GitHub Actions and publish them on our website. We source these directly from the original sites, ensuring the accuracy and security of each file. However, we are not responsible for any viruses or malicious software contained in the applications downloaded from our site; we only provide the links to the original sources. User security is our top priority, and we ensure transparency and automation at every step. Our aim is to automate all processes to deliver the fastest and most reliable experience. We are continuously evolving to offer our users the most up-to-date, trustworthy, and best applications.
